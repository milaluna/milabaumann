Soundscapes

'What if sound would be seen as a building material? Could we make invisible borders or spaces, only to be distinguished by ear?'
Previous Next

This project focusses on a walk from the Royal Acadamy of Arts to the Royal Conservatory of The Hague. While doing this walk I found myself categorising it in spaces. Specific turns or crossings became the border of each space. I became interested in this ‘invisible border’ and linked it to a phenomena in sound which allows you to aim it at a specific location. In this installation I tried to capture the sound between two half spheres. By moving your head through this invisible space the sound will grow louder or softer.

Presented at the exhibition “From here to there”.
