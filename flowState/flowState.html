A manifest for the body-mind relationship

Flow state is a space where the body-mind relationship is exposed. It gives you the opportunity to explore and to learn.

While it is a place to learn, Flow State doesnâ€™t provide an answer for how your body-mind relationship should be. After all I can only draw on my own knowledge and experience, which is very personal and highly biased. What Flow State does offer is a place where you can encounter the body-mind relationship.

Initially the installation functions as a discussion piece. It allows us to experience and address something that is usually hidden by habits. This is how it will function in the movie and during the exhibition.

In the long run it can become like a shrine for the body-mind relationship. It can be a space that helps us to encounter it and explore it. Like what improvisation does for me Flow State can become for others. A place we feel save to experiment and that we can go back to whenever we feel curious. And once you feel ready you can take this exploration outside of Flow State and into your daily life.
