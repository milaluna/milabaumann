Perfect Skin

'Behind a make-up wall we hide ourselves. Hoping to see something more... perfect'

Make-up might make us feel more secure and beautiful but it also limits the variety of colors and the amount of detail in our faces. While our skin provides us with a wide range of tones and subtle changes in color, make-up is constant and limited to what the market provides. The more make-up you use the more abstract your face becomes.
