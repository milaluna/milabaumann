A Dance of Flowerbulbs

'Bring up earth from the depths, smash out the weeds, spread it out on the land. A brutal dance creating beautiful patterns.'

This project talks about the flower bulb industry in Hillegom. When first visiting this landscape I was fascinated by the patterns left by machines working the land. They were like the score of a dance choreography, telling you about a sequence of movements. As I delved deeper into the industry this choreography got more and more layers. Each layer carries a different tone leaving us to question whether we saw something beautiful or brutal.
