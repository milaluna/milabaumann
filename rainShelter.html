Rain Shelter

'The world caught in a droplet of water. Blurring to a beautiful mixture of color and light.''

Rain Shelter by day

Rain Shelter by night

Previous Next

Rain is a wonderful thing. Rain sooths you to sleep at night. Raining on the top of your roof. Tick tick tick tick tick tick. Simultaneously you hear the rain on your window. Follow the drops on their way down. Two drops become one, a third drop merges in. A trail of little drops is left behind. Each drop containing a distorted reflection of the world, colors and lights outside. Step outside into the evening cityscape. Lights jump from red to green, cars drive past a sea of white and red. In the water pools on the ground all the colors come together and form a painting of reflected light, coloring the world red, white and green.

The rain shelter is meant to protect you from the rain while you can enjoy this blurring of colors, the rhythmic dripping of the rain and the raindrops sliding above and alongside you.
