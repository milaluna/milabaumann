Propriosception research

'Your center of gravity initiates movement, can you control it, use it. Will you explore how to collaborate with this object?'

In duo acrobatics I constantly change my center of gravity, initiated either by me or my base. Through the whole choreography my center of gravity is flowing through my body, occasionally resting in one place. The place the bodies of the base and the flyer meet, the place I am carried.

Based on this experience I created an installation which reacts to your weight. Depending on which bodypart you make heaviest the ropes will fall down or move up. Through collaboration and trust you can work together with the object and learn more about the way your body moves.
