About

'Design is where it all comes together: it is the practice of shaping our surroundings which we perceive with both body and mind'

Mila is a designer, teacher and performer.

In 2020 she graduated from KABK, Royal Academy of Art in The Hague.
Since then she has been working as an assistent designer, while also teaching circus and looking for opportunities to develop her own work. Having such different work fields makes her interested in mixing disciplines. Her knowledge as a designer makes her push for more creativity in the curriculum of circus, while her knowledge as a circus teacher and performer makes her interested in exploring movement and interaction within design. In the end the discipline becomes irrelevant.
